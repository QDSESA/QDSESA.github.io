Global Network

Since Qingdao Southeast Shipping Agency's main focus is shipping, we cooperate with trustworthy and renowed partners throughout the world. We keep in touch with them permanently and together we find optimal solutions for our customers.

Interested in becoming our partner, please contact the overseas department at: overseasqd@npsel.com

All these good relations support cost and time saving in our daily vessel operations and therefore guarantee efficient working favored to the principals' shipments.
