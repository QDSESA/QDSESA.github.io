About NPSEL-QD


Qingdao Southeast Shipping Agency Co., Ltd is part of the renowed Ningbo Port Southeast Logistics Group Co., Ltd, therefore we are able to offer the advantage of having access to nearly all ports throughout China.

We are pleased to offer our customers the oppertunity to transport all your freights by air, sea and truck by making use of our Class A-signed carriers YML, CMA, WANHAI, KMTC, PANOCEAN, EVERGREEN and HYUNDAI. Amongst others, our VIP clients are HAIDER, DHL, UPS, SCHENKER and YOUNGONE. Additionally we are doing continious improvements of all our services to serve our clients professionally throughout the world. 

We are offering: international freight forwarding by sea/air, NVOCC, shipping agency, warehousing, door pick-up trucking, customs declaration and clearance, bulk cargo, buyers consilidation and groupage, rail-sea combined multi-trans, export and import of cargo, etc.

Whether moving small consigments of products within/to/from/via China, we ensure the seamless movement of any cargo. Further, making use of our cooperation with other companies we are able to offer our services throughout the world.
