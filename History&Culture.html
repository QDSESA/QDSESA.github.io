History & Culture

From Visionary to Leader

Our company was founded in the port city of Ningbo, where the first cargo container was shipped from Ningbo old port to Hong Kong in October 1984. From now on, the spirit of vission and mission has been omnipressent. Southeast Group was established as Sinotrans Ningbo Southeast International. Freight Forwarding Company in September 1992 in Zhejiang province. By 1996 the company had become the biggest freight forwarder of container exports in Zhejiang province. Later on, in 2008, the company formed a Joint Venture with Ningbo Port Co., Ltd becoming a comprehensive logistics supplier, which continues its legacy until today. Nowadays, Southeast Logistics Group owns over 20 affiliated branches like Qingdao Southeast Shipping Agency Co., Ltd and is becoming one of the biggest ocean freight logistics enterprises in Zhejiang province with more than 700 employees.
